Dedicated Testing Facility

Your continued health and well-bring is our priority while you stay with us. The Real Code Ranch carries out regular COVID-19 tests with all of its ranchers, and you'll have peace of mind that precise contact-tracing is carried out in the event of any rancher testing positive.

In-house Medical Facility

The Real Code Ranch has its own private medical facility on-site. In addition to day-to-day medical care, our multi-lingual staff are trained to administer and process COVID-19 tests.

Asymptomatic Quarantine System

In the event that you test positive for COVID-19 at The Real Code Ranch, your holiday doesn't stop. For asymptomatic patients, we have luxurious self-isolation solutions available. Networking sessions can be attended remotely, and you can take in the beauty of the Croatian coast from the garden of your detached villa.

Symptomatic Quarantine System

For symptomatic patients we offer one of our clifftop penthouses and a dedicated nurse from our medical facility. Watch the catamarans from your private isolation balcony and plan your next excursion while you convalesce with a cocktail.
