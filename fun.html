Explore Croatia

The Real Code Ranch offers its customers a number of outdoors activities to explore Croatia and recharge after camp.

Horse-Riding

The Real Code Ranch has its own stables with family-friendly horses for all sizes and skill levels. Riders receive personal protective equipment and training before leaving the ranch. There's no better place to experience Croatia's wild landscapes and feel the spark of adventure than from the saddle.

Historic Cities

The Real Code Ranch's central location lends itself to bus tours of historic centers of trade on the Adriatic sea dating to the middle ages. Private ranch buses leave the ranch for Dubrovnik every two hours, with optional tours for all customers. Visit the historic Walls of Dubrovnik and imposing the Fort of Lovrijenac - both recognisable as locations in Game of Thrones.

A Change of Pace

If the hustle and bustle of cities isn't to your taste, The Real Code Ranch offers its private chalets in the sleepy island town of Trogir. Trogir is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, whose winding streets are lined with Romanesque, Renaissance, and Baroque architecture.

Sailing Adventures

The wind in your sails and the fresh sea air - is there anything better? The Real Code Ranch has yachts available for charter that are crewed by experienced mariners. We offer anything from 1 cabin sailboats to a 40 ft catamaran. Be the captain of your own ship on the beautiful Adriatic.

Beautiful Beaches

Croatia has some of the most beautiful and pristine beaches in the world. Dotted all along the Adriatic coast, these beauty spots can be found nestled among cliffs, in the secluded bays of islands, and jutting out into the waters of the Adriatic.
